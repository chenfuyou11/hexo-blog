# 25个常用动词的短语

divided by preposition/adverb.

通式: _v. + prep./adv._

动词含义弱化: 介词(对象), 副词(方向)

副词: apart, aside, as, away, back, forward
介词: against, at, for, from, of, into
介副均可: across, about, after, around, by, down, on/off, in
